Delete Your Data

How to delete your personal data from Hydriq

Your data, your control. All Hydriq data is stored locally on your device in an encrypted database. Nothing is sent to our servers. You can delete any or all of your data at any time using the steps below.

Option 1: Delete All Data

This permanently removes all hydration logs, profile information, achievements, and preferences.

1. Open Hydriq

2. Tap the Settings tab (bottom navigation)

3. Tap Data & Privacy

4. Scroll down and tap Delete All Data

5. Confirm the deletion in the dialog

This action is irreversible. All locally stored data will be permanently erased and the app will reset to its initial state.

Option 2: Delete Cloud Backups

If you enabled Google Drive backup, copies of your data are stored in your Google Drive's app-isolated folder. To delete them:

1. Open Hydriq → Settings → Data & Privacy

2. Tap Delete Cloud Backups

3. Confirm the deletion

This removes all Hydriq backup files from your Google Drive. Cloud backups are stored in a hidden appDataFolder that is only accessible by Hydriq — they are not visible in your Google Drive UI.

Option 3: Delete Individual Entries

To remove specific drink logs without deleting everything:

1. On the Home tab, find the entry in the Entries section

2. Swipe the entry left to delete it

3. Tap Undo on the Snackbar if you deleted by mistake

Option 4: Uninstall the App

Uninstalling Hydriq from your device removes all locally stored data, including the encrypted database, preferences, and local backup files. No data remains on your device after uninstall.

Note: If you enabled Google Drive backup, cloud copies are not automatically deleted when you uninstall. Use Option 2 above to delete cloud backups before uninstalling, or revoke Hydriq's access via Google Account Permissions.

Data Retention

Hydriq does not retain any data after deletion. There are no server-side copies, no analytics logs, and no backup retention periods. When you delete your data using any of the methods above, it is permanently removed.
